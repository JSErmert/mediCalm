# MediCalm Copy System  
  
Status: Draft  
Owner: Josh  
Purpose: Centralize the app's on-screen language so tone stays medically grounded, calm, concise, and consistent across intake, guidance, safety, completion, and history.  
  
## Copy Rules  
  
All copy should feel:  
- calm  
- clear  
- concise  
- medically grounded  
- non-judgmental  
- non-hyped  
  
Avoid:  
- guarantees  
- dramatic emotional language  
- mystical or spiritual claims  
- dense clinical jargon  
- motivational hype  
- vague wellness filler  
  
## Brand Tone Summary  
  
MediCalm speaks like:  
- a calm medical support system  
- a guided low-overload interface  
- a precise but gentle companion  
  
## Intake Copy  
- `What level is your pain right now?`  
- `Where do you feel it?`  
- `What kind of pain is it?`  
- `What seems to trigger it?`  
- `Start session`  
- `Continue`  
- `Back`  
  
## Guided Session Copy  
  
### Start Guidance  
- `Start here.`  
- `Follow this.`  
  
### Step Examples  
- `Inhale four. Expand ribs.`  
- `Exhale seven. Drop shoulders.`  
- `Jaw loose. Neck soft.`  
- `Lean forward. Support your body.`  
- `Use less effort right now.`  
  
### Pause / Reduce Effort  
- `Slow down here.`  
- `Use less effort.`  
- `Do not force this.`  
  
### Gentle Reinforcement  
- `Good. Continue.`  
- `Stay with this.`  
- `Keep it easy.`  
  
## Safety Copy  
  
### Caution  
- `Stop here.`  
- `Do not continue this session.`  
- `Rest and reassess.`  
  
### Seek Care Soon  
- `These symptoms may need medical evaluation.`  
- `Do not continue another session right now.`  
  
### Seek Urgent Care  
- `Stop now.`  
- `Do not continue in the app.`  
- `Seek urgent medical care.`  
  
### Prohibited Safety Language  
- `You are definitely safe.`  
- `Nothing is wrong.`  
- `You do not need medical care.`  
  
## Completion Copy  
- `Session complete.`  
- `Notice what changed.`  
- `Save your response.`  
- `Helped`  
- `No clear change`  
- `Worse`  
- `Interrupted`  
  
## Follow-Up  
- `You may continue carefully.`  
- `Use one more low-risk step.`  
- `Stop here and reassess.`  
  
## Home / History Copy  
- `Recent sessions`  
- `Review what helped`  
- `Return to a previous session`  
- `Session details`  
- `Sequence used`  
- `Breath timing`  
- `Your response`  
  
## Settings Copy  
- `Ambient audio`  
- `Reduced motion`  
- `Haptics`  
- `About MediCalm`  
- `Medical boundaries`  
  
## About / Boundary Copy  
- `MediCalm provides guided symptom-support sessions.`  
- `It does not diagnose or replace medical care.`  
- `If symptoms worsen or feel concerning, seek medical care.`  
  
## Words to Avoid  
- cure  
- heal instantly  
- guaranteed  
- miracle  
- fix permanently  
- medically validated protocol engine  
  
## Claude Code Implementation Notes  
  
- Reuse exact phrases from this file wherever possible.  
- Do not improvise a new brand voice in implementation.  
- Prefer brevity over explanation during active discomfort states.  
- Let safety copy remain firmer than normal guidance copy.
